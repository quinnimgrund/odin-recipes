Tangy Honey-Glazed Ham

Home
Honey-glazed ham that will blow your face clean off (complimentary).

I was grieving the death of my grandmother, and decided I needed comfort food, like mac and cheese...but I followed the wrong recipe. Here's how to make honey-glazed ham, I guess.

Ingredients

  • 1 (10 pound) fully-cooked, bone-in ham
  • 1 ¼ cups packed dark brown sugar
  • ⅓ cup pineapple juice
  • ⅓ cup honey, or more to taste
  • ⅓ large orange
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 ½ teaspoons lemon juice, or to taste (Optional)
